Are you Born Again?

Justified by Faith

We were born Christians. As we call people who are not Christians, we were not sinners at birth. But we are aware that abiding by the law does not make one righteous in God's eyes. Trusting in Jesus Christ is what reconciles a person to God. Since we want to be reconciled to God, we placed our confidence in Christ Jesus. And it is because of our faith in Christ, not because we kept the law, that we are right with him. I am able to say this because obeying the law cannot make someone right with God. It is obvious that we Jews were also sinners since we came to Christ to be reconciled to God. Does this imply that we are sinners because of Christ? Of course not.
